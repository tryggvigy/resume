Swimming

Competitive

I have been swimming competitively since I was 10 years old. I have competed on many international tournaments. My main stroaks are breaststroke and butterfly.

When I train at maximum intensity, I go nine times a week in the pool as well as three dry-land workouts. Morning practices start at 5:30 in the morning. Swimming is a hard but rewarding sport.
